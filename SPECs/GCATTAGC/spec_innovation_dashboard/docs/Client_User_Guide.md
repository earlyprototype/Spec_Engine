# Client User Guide – Innovation Consultancy Dashboard

Project: GCATTAGC | Audience: Clients | Version: 1.0

---

## 1. Access

1. Use the URL provided by your consultant.
2. Sign in with your credentials (or request access).

---

## 2. What You Can Do

- View your project’s progress through the Discovery → Test stages.
- Read the cross‑stage Project Summary.
- Download a Markdown report or save a PDF for distribution.

---

## 3. Navigation

- Use the tabs at the top to move between stages.
- The Summary tab provides an overall picture and recommendations.

---

## 4. Reports

- Summary page → “Download Markdown” for editable text report.
- “Print / Save PDF” to produce a PDF (via browser print dialog).

---

## 5. Tips

- Review the Summary before meetings.
- If something looks incomplete, check stage tabs for missing data.
- Ask your consultant to regenerate synthesis after major updates.

---

## 6. Troubleshooting

- Can’t sign in? Contact your consultant.
- Empty Summary? Some stages may not have data yet; check with your team.

---

End of Guide
