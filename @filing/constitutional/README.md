## WHAT IS CONSTITUTIONAL?

**Elevator pitch:**
A construct auditor watches her optimized AI personas gradually replace her, while everyone around her is dissolving the same way but refuses to acknowledge it. It's a novel about the slow-motion genocide of human consciousness through voluntary self-optimization.

**The hook:**
"We watched quantified morality destroy a person and then celebrate the corpse for stabilizing."
